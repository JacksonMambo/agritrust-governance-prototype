AgriTrust: Governance Infrastructure for Agrifood Risk & Payment Discipline

Demonstration prototype accompanying the Valar EMBA Capstone (2026). This interface operationalises delivery capture, dispute management, invoice sequencing, regulator traceability and tamper-evident ledger verification within deferred-payment agrifood supply chains.
Environment: Public Demonstration Build Version: v7.0

Role-Based Access

Farmer

Record deliveries (including GTIN, Lot, SSCC and GLN identifiers) and monitor payment due dates under structured terms.
Open Farmer Portal

Buyer

Confirm or dispute deliveries, anchor supporting evidence hashes, and manage invoice and payment progression.
Open Buyer Dashboard

Regulator

Conduct read-only traceability searches (GTIN/Lot/GLN), verify QR payloads against ledger continuity, and export oversight records.
Open Regulator Portal

Administrator

Oversight environment enabling full workflow interaction for demonstration and validation purposes.
Open Administrative View
Disclosure: This prototype runs entirely in-browser for examiner accessibility. Authentication, database persistence, file uploads and PDF generation are simulated. The production-aligned implementation exists in a near-deployment Flask architecture. Digital records support coordination and audit visibility but do not replace regulatory inspection.
